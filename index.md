### The scientific journey

Conny obtained her PhD at [CUHK](https://www.cuhk.edu.hk/) (Hong Kong). After her postdoctoral training at [UCL](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/) (London) and [St Andrews](https://www.st-andrews.ac.uk/) (Fife), she joined the [MRC-LMB](https://mrclmb.ac.uk/) (Cambridge) as a staff structural biologist. She has collaborated widely and published in multiple fields, including cell division, RNA transcription and protein translation. Since 2024, she has transitioned to data science and now works as a data curator for UniProt at [EMBL-EBI](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/) (Cambridge).

Her work focuses on bridging structural insights with the broader biological community and translating protein structures into functional understanding.
